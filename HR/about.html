ABOUT

Hitutor, the Subsidiary of Dewey International Education Consultants Ltd., is Taiwan’s first and largest online multi-language learning organization.

Foreseeing learning language online would be a trend in near future, Dewey makes a great use of its educational background and combines large-scale resources with technology to establish a reputable and professional online language academy. Established in 2009, Hitutor provides the highest quality online language learning service for learners in Taiwan and opens a new era of online education. Today, we offer beyond 50 themed professional and interesting courses with over 15 languages for more than 30,000 learners to learn online worldwide.

With Brilliant Academic R&D team and top IT experts, Hitutor has not only built convenient learning environment for learners, also a strong support for our elite teachers to present teaching expertise.

Learners can learn languages anywhere with a laptop. With a headset and microphone, teachers can easily conduct classes online. Flexible teaching hours, Clear company policies, and with most qualified specialists to assist teachers anytime. We are always seeking for talented, elite teachers to join Hitutor.

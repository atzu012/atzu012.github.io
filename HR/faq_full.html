Working Hours

  • Do I need a TESOL/TEFL certificate to apply to teach with Hitutor?

    For teachers who want to apply, a TESOL/ TEFL certificate will be plus.
    If you didn't hold a TESOL/TEFL certificateyou can provide other same level language exam certificates will do as well.

  • What will my teaching schedule be?

    Teachers can open time slot 24 hrs per day, and 7 days a week according to their available time. Our schedule is flexible to meet each teacher’s lifestyle.

  • Who will I be teaching?

    Most of our students are Taiwanese, Chinese and Hong Konges. From elementary school age to professionals/ adults.

  • What course's type do Hitutor offer?

    HiTutor offer different variety type of courses to meet students' needs, such as, Business English, Exam course,Writing Course, etc.
    We also provide Asian languages course and European languges course and other course.

  • What equipment should I prepare if I wanted to be Home-based teacher?

    A computer/ laptop and a good sound effect headset are required.
    Stable Internet connection.

    (upload: 2 MB / download: 5 MB required)

  • Where will I conduct classes?

    At HOME!
    That's one of the benefit to work at HiTutor, you can conduct classes at home and earn income without going thru traffic headache.
